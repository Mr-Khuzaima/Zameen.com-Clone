About Us

Zameen.com is Pakistan's Largest Online Real Estate Portal
Connecting Buyers with Sellers within & outside the country

How did it all start?

Zameen.com was the brainchild of two British-Pakistani entrepreneur brothers, Zeeshan Ali Khan and Imran Ali Khan. Their mission was to make Pakistan real estate accessible and convenient for everyone. Together, they proceeded to lead Zameen.com from a small start-up to the premier real estate entity in the country.

The real journey truly found its footing in 2012, which consisted of several angel investors and multinational investment venture capital firms. A further two rounds of major investments were closed in late 2014 and early 2015, bringing in a total of $29 million.
